 Bike Sales System
This project is a web-based system for managing a bike shop, including inventory and sales management.

Features

Inventory Management
- Register new bikes with details: name, type, chassis number, purchase price, and quantity.  
- Search functionality to find bikes in the inventory.  
- Inventory table showing bike names, types, chassis numbers, purchase prices, available quantities, and stock status (e.g., full or low stock).  
- Ability to **edit** or **delete** any information in the inventory.

 Sales Management
- Record customer purchases with details: customer information, bike type, purchase date, and sale price.  
- The system **prevents selling a bike at the same price or lower than its purchase price**.  
- Table displaying recent sales with options to **edit**, **delete**, **export**, and **print** records.

Daily Revenue
- Table for tracking daily income with columns for date and revenue.  
- Compare system revenue with actual cash on hand.  
- Ability to **export** and **print** daily revenue reports.

 Technologies Used
- HTML  
- CSS  
- JavaScript
